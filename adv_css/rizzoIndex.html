Rizzo

Rizzo the rat is a streetwise, New Jersey-accented rodent. He's been Gonzo's sidekick since 1992 when he made something of a theatrical debut in the 'The Muppet Christmas Carol,' which I am going to continue to reiterate is the best Christmas Carol, and perhaps best movie of all time. (Previous submission of "The Princess Bride" is herein duly noted and incorporated by reference. )

Notable characteristics, achievements, and roles.

  • Has 1,274 brothers and sisters. This is a sample. Really, you don't want to to see more...
  • Based on very gritty 'Midnight Cowboy' role
  • Crucial Gonzo sidekick, esp, in re: 'The Muppets Christmas Carol'
