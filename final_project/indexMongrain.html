Erik Mongrain and Boxing the Acoustic Guitar

Erik Mongrain

When this french gentleman emerged on the scene, I can only think of one other acoustic guitarist who used both hands on the guitar and also used the guitar body to achieve acoustic effects as well. Those poor guitars! They were just innocently sitting there waiting to make beautiful music...they had no idea regarding the fisticuffs to come.
