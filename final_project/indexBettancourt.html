Nuno, The Wounded Bumblebee, and That Metronome

Nuno Bettancourt

Nuno Bettancourt is the guitarist for a band called Extreme. I'm not going to lie here; I don't think some extreme songs are real works of art in and of themselves, but there's no arguing that he's a master guitarist. The clip below shows him racing a metronome in one of his trademark Live solo songs, as well as a medley of Extreme instrumental interpretations. Personally, I always wanted to pick up one of his trademark Washburn guitars but never really had the spare cash.
