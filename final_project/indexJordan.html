Stanley Jordan The Two-Hand Tapping Jazzman

Stanley Jordan

There was a time in the distant past when two-hand tapping - largely made popular by Eddie Van Halen - wasn't a very common technique. It's now kind of a standard in the rock and heavy metal guitarists toolkit. So, imagine our surpise sometime in the late 80s when this jazz-playing gentle emerges not only two-hand tapping but playing entire songs in two parts with both hands. And then he goes ahead and decides he needs to play more than one guitar at once. Bananas.
