Paul Gilbert, Racer X, and The Drill

Paul Gilbert

Paul Gilbert has been a huge influence on all sorts of guitar players. Back in the day, he was one of the two guitarists in a band called Racer X. Both guitarists in the band were graduates of the Guitar Institute of Technology, which was a big deal at the time. Their music included ultra-fast tempos, string-skipping acrobatics, sweep arpeggios, and the theatrical use of a power drill to pick. Some said this was an homage to Jimmy Page having used a violin bow or that it was the only way he could pick fast enough to keep up with the fingers on his left hand.
