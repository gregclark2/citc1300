Gonzo the Weirdo

After decades of speculation about Gonzo's interest in chickens, it was revealed in the movie, "Muppets from Space" that Gonzo is a 'weirdo' from a race of extraterrestrial weirdos. Now, this explains exactly nothing, but made for a very dramatic spaceship scene. Among the questions unanswered are: (1) Why do you love chickens so much? and (2) Why is Camilla your Kardashian?

Notable characteristics, achievements, and roles.

  • Superb Dickens Narrator
  • Daredevil Extraordinaire
  • Troublesome Chicken Issue...yeah, no one knows what that's about...
